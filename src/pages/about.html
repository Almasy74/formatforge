FormatForge is a technical workshop designed for developers, data engineers, and "tech operators" who need precise, reliable tools for handling structured data.

Who Operates This Site?

FormatForge is built and maintained by an **independent developer based in Norway**. Unlike many online tool directories that prioritize ad-revenue over utility, this project was born out of a personal need for a "clean room" environment to fix broken JSON, clean legacy CSVs, and audit data pipes without the fear of data logging.

Our Philosophy: Privacy-First Utility

The core principle of FormatForge is **local processing**. We believe that your data is yours alone. That is why 99% of our tools operate entirely within your web browser. When you paste sensitive API responses or customer data into our workshop, that data never touches a server. It stays in your RAM, is transformed by our client-side logic, and disappears when you close the tab.

The FormatForge Mission

  1. Utility Over Marketing: We don't write generic "SEO fluff." Our documentation is designed to solve real debugging cases.
  2. Technical Precision: We focus on edge cases—helping you fix trailing commas, Unicode artifacts, and schema mismatches.
  3. Zero Friction: No accounts, no subscriptions, no data-walls. Just tools that work.

Thank you for using the workshop. If you have suggestions for new tools or improvements, feel free to reach out.
